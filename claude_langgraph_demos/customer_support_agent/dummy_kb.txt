Anthropic Support Knowledge Base

Account Issues
To delete your account, please visit the settings page and click "Delete Account". Note that this action is irreversible.
If you are having trouble logging in, please check your email for a verification code.
If your account has been banned, you can submit an appeal through our support portal.

Billing
We accept major credit cards and PayPal. Invoices are generated at the end of each billing cycle.
Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days.
You can update your payment method in the Billing section of your dashboard.

Technical Support
If you are experiencing downtime, please check our status page at status.anthropic.com.
For API integration issues, please refer to our documentation.
If you encounter a bug, please report it via the "Report a Bug" button in the interface.

Legal & Privacy
We take data privacy seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for more information.
Our Terms of Service outline the acceptable use of our services.

Features
Claude allows you to upload documents for analysis.
You can use the API to integrate Claude into your own applications.
Team plans allow for shared workspaces and centralized billing.
