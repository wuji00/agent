Product: Acme Cloud Storage
Acme Cloud Storage is a secure, cloud-based storage solution for individuals and businesses.
Features include automatic backup, file sharing, and version history.
Pricing:
- Basic: Free, 5GB storage
- Pro: $10/month, 1TB storage
- Business: $25/month/user, unlimited storage

Technical Support:
If you are experiencing slow upload speeds, please check your internet connection.
For login issues, ensure your caps lock is off and you are using the correct email address.
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
Our servers undergo maintenance every Sunday from 2 AM to 4 AM UTC.

Billing:
Invoices are sent via email on the 1st of every month.
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal.
To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.

Returns/Refunds:
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for Pro and Business plans.
To request a refund, contact support@acme.com within 30 days of purchase.
