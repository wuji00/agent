Anthropic is an AI safety and research company that creates reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems.

Our flagship product is Claude, a next-generation AI assistant capable of a wide variety of tasks.

Current Models:
- Claude 3 Opus: Our most powerful model, delivering state-of-the-art performance on highly complex tasks, including open-ended generation, strategy, and research.
- Claude 3 Sonnet: The ideal balance of intelligence and speed for enterprise workloads, including data processing, RAG, and coding.
- Claude 3 Haiku: Our fastest and most compact model for near-instant responsiveness, perfect for customer interactions and content moderation.

API Usage:
You can access Claude via the Anthropic API.
Authentication involves setting the `x-api-key` header.
The current API version is `2023-06-01`.

Account Management:
- To reset your password, visit the login page and click "Forgot Password".
- To delete your account, please contact privacy@anthropic.com.
- Billing information can be updated in the "Settings" > "Billing" section of the console.
